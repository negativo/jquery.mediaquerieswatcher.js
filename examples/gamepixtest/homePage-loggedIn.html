THE STORY BEGINS

in GALAXY FAR, FAR AWAY...

From the farthest galaxy ever seen, Pix and Xip bumped into our solar system. Attracted by the bright colours and flashing lights of Planet Earth, Pix and Xip decided to settle in and divided the whole planet in millions of small pixels. But unfortunately, the day after their landing, Pix woke up and found himself trapped in a dull and polluted gray world: the world dominated by his brother Xip. 

Help Pix in his struggle to give colours back to the planet!
